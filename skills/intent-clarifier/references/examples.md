# Examples

These examples are synthetic and contain no user data. Adapt their depth to the request rather than copying the wording. The broader evaluation corpus also covers accessibility, caregiving, volunteer, operational, and adversarial contexts without claiming to represent every user or culture.

## Vague Goal, No Question Needed

**Input**

> 我想做一个个人项目，但工作很忙，又不想半途而废。

**Output**

### 目标

选择一个能在有限业余时间内完成并验证价值的小型个人项目。

### 多层次需求

- 明确诉求：确定做什么项目。
- 期望结果：真正完成一次，而不是长期停留在构想阶段。
- 核心优先级：可持续性高于功能规模，这是基于“工作很忙”的推断。
- 成功标准：两周内产生一个可以展示或使用的结果。

### 个性化约束

- 必须满足：不依赖大块连续时间。
- 偏好与取舍：接受较小范围，换取完成概率。
- 假设：每周可以稳定投入两到三次短时段。

### 直接回答

选择一个两周微项目：只解决一个你本人每周都会遇到的问题，并把首版限制为一个输入、一个核心处理步骤和一个可见结果。先写下完成定义，再选择具体题目。

## Conflicting Priorities

**Input**

> I need a laptop for frequent travel, local development, and occasional video editing. I care about battery life but do not want to overspend.

**Output**

### Goal

Choose a travel-friendly development laptop that handles occasional editing without paying for unused performance.

### Layered needs

- Explicit request: Select a laptop.
- Desired outcome: Reliable mobile development with acceptable editing performance.
- Core priority: Battery life and portability appear more important than maximum render speed.
- Success signal: A full workday away from power and smooth editing for occasional projects.

### Personalized constraints

- Must satisfy: Portable, strong battery life, suitable for local development.
- Preference and trade-off: Accept slower exports to avoid workstation-level cost and weight.
- Unknown: Operating-system requirement and budget ceiling.

### Direct answer

Start with a current thin-and-light model using at least 16 GB of memory and 512 GB of storage. Do not pay for the highest GPU tier unless video editing becomes weekly work. Set the budget ceiling before comparing individual models.

## One Material Question

**Input**

> Help me choose between changing careers now or waiting another year.

**Response**

The decision turns on whether waiting creates a concrete advantage rather than simply reducing discomfort. What specific resource would you gain in the next year: savings, a credential, a portfolio, or a confirmed opportunity?

After the answer, provide the goal, relevant constraints, and a direct recommendation. Do not start a second questionnaire.

## User Requests No Questions

**Input**

> 不要问我问题，直接帮我把“想提升英语”说清楚。

**Output**

目标：在日常工作场景中更顺畅地理解和表达，而不是笼统地“学好英语”。假设你当前最需要的是实际使用能力，优先选择一个高频场景，例如会议发言或英文资料阅读，并用四周内可观察的表现作为成功标准。
